Welcome to the Pennsylvania Center for Dental Excellence!

Dr. Leonard Tau, dentist in Philadelphia, is committed to providing you with excellent dentistry in a comfortable environment. You deserve a skilled, experienced dental team that can help you ensure great oral health. Experience what patient-centered care can do for you. We welcome new patients of all ages.

Your Care Should Be as Individual as You Are

Your dental experience is fully personalized at The Pennsylvania Center for Dental Excellence. Our friendly, knowledgeable team will address every question and concern. Your oral health needs, goals, and priorities are the focus of your customized treatment plan. We want to work with you to create a healthy, unforgettable smile.

Look and Feel Better with Cosmetic Dentistry Solutions

A beautiful, healthy smile can improve your confidence, self-esteem, and overall health. Our cosmetic dental solutions include porcelain dental veneers, cosmetic bonding, teeth whitening, and more. Improve your quality of life by achieving the smile of your dreams. Visit our Services page for more information.

You Benefit from our Education and Experience

Northeast Philly dentist, Dr. Tau, has an ongoing commitment to continuing education. We invest countless hours in learning the latest techniques and technologies in dentistry. You will receive effective and efficient care from our experienced dental team. Make The Pennsylvania Center for Dental Excellence your dental care partner.
