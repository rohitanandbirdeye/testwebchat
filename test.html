Team

Kate McElroy, PA-C

Center Manager, Marin

Kate McElroy, PA-C

Center Manager

Kate has a history of working in the health wellness and fitness industries, and practices preemptive medicine with a focus on nutrition education, lifestyle modification/behavior change, and positive psychology. Through the management of a variety of chronic health conditions including being overweight, obesity, diabetes, hypertension, and hyperlipidemia, she concentrates on the overarching goal of sustained weight loss and heightens physical and mental well-being. Kate is a Certified Physician Assistant and Harvard Certified Lifestyle Medicine Coach and a graduate of Stanford University.

Frances Strzempko Ahmad, PhD, NP-C

Clinician,
Marin

Frances Strzempko Ahmad

Clinician

Fran brings diverse experiences to her role at JumpstartMD, having guided her entire career with a patient-centered philosophy. She has a passion for improving peoples lives in a holistic and natural way through bioidentical hormones and lifestyle change. Fran prides herself on being able to provide a safe space for discussing intimate matters with a style that is comfortable yet professional. She received her BS and MS from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst and her PhD and Nurse Practitioner training at the University of California, San Francisco. She has worked as a perinatal and labor and delivery nurse, has taught undergraduate nursing as a clinical faculty member, and had her doctoral research on the impact of endometriosis on the couple relationship funded by NIH. She is also a Board Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner.
