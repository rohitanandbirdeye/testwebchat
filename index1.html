A MASTERMIND GROUP FOR FEMALE ENTREPRENEURS

    WHO WE ARE

    Bosses & Breadwinners is a mastermind group for female entrepreneurs who want to take their lives and businesses to the next level. Each chapter is intentionally small, allowing authentic connections to form. We meet once per week to brainstorm ideas, solve problems, overcome limiting beliefs, and celebrate success. If you're searching for a tribe of #businessbesties, Bosses & Breadwinners is for you!
